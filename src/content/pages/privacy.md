## Introduction

This website is a personal portfolio operated by an individual developer.
Your privacy is respected, and this site aims to collect as little information as possible.

This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when you visit the website and how it is used.

## Information That May Be Collected

When you visit this website, certain limited technical information may be collected automatically. This may include:

- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Device type
- Pages visited
- Date and time of visits

This information is used only to understand general website usage and maintain the reliability and security of the site.

The website does not provide user accounts, collect personal information through forms, or store personal data in a database.

## Analytics

This website uses Vercel Analytics to understand general usage patterns, such as which pages are visited and how users navigate the site.

Vercel Analytics is designed to be privacy-friendly and focuses on aggregated usage data rather than identifying individual users.

## Hosting

This website is hosted by Vercel.
As part of providing hosting and content delivery services, Vercel may process technical information such as IP addresses and request logs.

For more information, please see Vercel’s privacy policy:
[https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy](https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy)

## Cookies

This website does not intentionally use cookies for tracking or advertising. However, some infrastructure or analytics services may use minimal cookies or similar technologies to operate correctly.

## Third-Party Services

The website relies on third-party infrastructure providers to operate. These providers may process limited technical information necessary to deliver the website. No advertising networks or tracking services are used.

## Data Retention

This website does not store personal data about visitors. Any technical data processed by infrastructure or analytics services is retained only as necessary to operate and improve those services.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in how the website operates. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.

## Contact

This website is a personal project.
